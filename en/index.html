About me

From 2014 to 2021, I worked as a Frontend Developer in 🇦🇷 Su Web Express, one of the leading digital agencies in Argentina. In 2019 we created 🇺🇾 Urutienda, where I've been working as a WordPress Developer since then.

In 2022 I started teaching Web Development at the digital academy Coderhouse. Then, I created my YouTube channel as a place to upload extra content for my students, and it has evolved into what is today: a channel with a lot of videos and courses about HTML, CSS and JavaScript, reaching more than 2700 subscribers.

I recently moved to 🇪🇸 Spain, in search of challenges, opportunities and new projects.
